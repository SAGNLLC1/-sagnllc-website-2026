About Us

Built to Serve. Ready to Deliver.

SAGN LLC is a Miami-Dade based, woman-owned small business founded by Joanne Seepersad. We specialize in providing dependable general services and supply chain solutions to federal, state, and local government agencies.

From supply sourcing and procurement fulfillment to grounds maintenance coordination, SAGN LLC offers the agility of a small business with the professionalism that government contracts demand. We are SAM.gov registered, listed on MyFloridaMarketPlace, and prepared to accept purchase orders through standard government procurement channels.

As a new entrant in the government contracting space, we bring fresh energy, competitive pricing, and an owner-managed approach where accountability is never delegated. Our owner holds a background in commercial facility services, procurement operations, and administrative management, bringing practical experience to every contract we pursue.

We welcome teaming arrangements and mentor-protégé partnerships with established primes.
